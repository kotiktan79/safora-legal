2. What Safora does

Safora helps Shopify merchants prepare product-safety information required by the EU General Product Safety Regulation ("GPSR") and publish it on product pages. The App:

  • Reads merchant product data (titles, descriptions, metafields) via the Shopify Admin API
  • Optionally drafts certain fields (safety warnings, usage instructions, product identifier) using third-party AI services
  • Writes drafted or merchant-entered values back to Shopify product metafields
  • Renders those metafields on storefront product pages via a Shopify theme app extension

The App does not request access to customer personal data, and we do not store or process customer personal data in the course of providing the App's functionality.
