Safora

AI-fill EU GPSR product safety data and show it on every product page. For Shopify.

What it does

Safora is a Shopify app that helps EU-facing merchants meet General Product Safety Regulation (GPSR) requirements. AI drafts the safety information from your existing product data, you review and approve it, and the app shows it directly on every product page — where regulators look.

Why it matters

GPSR is strictly enforced in 2026. Marketplaces delist non-compliant products. Fines reach €10M. Doing the work by hand across hundreds of SKUs is slow and error-prone. Safora gets you there in minutes.
